Making early diagnosis a reality

We are working on an early warning system for Alzheimer’s disease that enables cost effective, non-invasive screening.

Our mission.

CVT’s mission is to make the data driven, standardised diagnosis of chronic retinal and neurodegenerative diseases available to all. Our first product is an early warning system for Alzheimer’s disease that enables cost effective, non-invasive population screening. This consists of a highly-scalable, portable, retinal imager and machine intelligence system capable of measuring Alzheimer’s biomarkers. It has the potential to improve the lives of over 50 million people over the next 10 years and save the world over $220 billion in dementia care every year.
