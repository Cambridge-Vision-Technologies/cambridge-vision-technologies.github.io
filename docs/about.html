Andrew Kadis

Andrew is a former technology consultant with expert-level experience in connected medical-device IOT applications. With 13 years of experience, he has developed wearable fitness trackers, was part of the team who developed the world’s first smart asthma inhaler for 3M. He has also managed complex R&D projects including a magnetic-tracking surgical system for Boston Scientific and a cellular-therapy cryogenic shipment system for GE Healthcare.

Milad Alemohammad

Milad is an expert-level optical engineer with multiple patents in advanced imaging technologies for applications in microscopy, high throughput materials characterisation, and brain imaging. He provides deep technical expertise having developed a host of customised high-performance microscopy systems at Johns Hopkins University and has built bespoke hyperspectral imaging and OCT systems. He is also a capable data scientist.

James Wood

James' company Data Transparency has provided healthcare software to the NHS since he founded it in 2006. His systems have processed over 100,000 patient records in the UK. James attended Columbia University’s MBA program in 2016 and since then architected the launch of PPAYA, the UK’s largest renewable energy trading platform.
